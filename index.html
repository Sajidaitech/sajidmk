    Project Title:Revolutionizing Online Education

    Problem Statement:

    High Licensing Costs: Many virtual classroom solutions require expensive licenses, making them inaccessible to smaller institutions and individual educators.

    Lack of Personalization: Traditional online learning platforms often fail to provide personalized support, leading to a one-size-fits-all approach that does not cater to individual learning needs.

    Limited Collaboration Tools: Many platforms lack seamless integration of real-time collaboration tools, hindering effective communication and interaction between educators and students.

    Sustainability Challenges: Most educational platforms rely on subscription fees, which can be prohibitive for some users, limiting their accessibility and long-term sustainability.

    Objectives:

    • To design and implement a web-based virtual classroom system with unlimited session capability.
    • To integrate AI-powered features for personalized learning assistance and student support.
    • To develop a donation-based payment system to ensure platform sustainability.
    • To create an intuitive user interface for both educators and students.
    • To implement real-time collaboration tools and interactive learning features.
    • To ensure the platform is accessible to users with varying levels of technical expertise.
    • To provide automated content recommendations based on individual learning patterns.
    • To incorporate intelligent tutoring systems for real-time feedback and guidance.
    • To evaluate the effectiveness of the platform through user testing and feedback.
    • To contribute to the broader goal of making quality education accessible to all.
